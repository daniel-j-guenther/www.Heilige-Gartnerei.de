Directions

From Berlin Central Station (Hauptbahnhof), We recommend using the 🚃 S3/S5/S7/S9 to get to Berlin Ostbahnhof. Now you can board the 🚄 RE1 - Frankfurt (Oder). Travel all the way until you get to Fürstenwalde (SpreE). find the 🚍434 BUS (be careful it only operates during weekdays) NOW ride until you reach Wilmersdorf (bei Briesen). If you want, someone from our team can meet you, or simply walk (7 minutes) until you get to House number 3.
